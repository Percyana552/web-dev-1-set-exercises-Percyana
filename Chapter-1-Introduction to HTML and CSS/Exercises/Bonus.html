Orange cake Recipe

A delicious Orange cake reciepe that's bursting with fresh circus flavor.

ingredients

  • 3 large eggs.
  • 1 cup granulated sugar.
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil (such as extra-virgin olive oil or canola oil).
  • 1 cup of fresh Orange.
  • Zest of 1 Orange
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

    Instructions

  1. Beat eggs and sugar: in an eletric mixer fitted with a wishk attachment( or using a handhed mixer), wisk together the eggs and sugar on high speed until pale and fluffy(about 5 minutes)properly beating the eggs ensures a light and fluffy crumb
  2. Mix Dry ingredients: in a separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking powder.set aside
  3. Add wet ingredients:slowly add the oil, Orange zest , and vanilla extract to the egg-sugar mixture.Mix on low speed until well combined
  4. Incorporate Dry ingredients:keeping the mixer on low, gradually add the flour is completely combined avoid overmixing
  5. Bake: Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan or a Bundt pan lined with parchment paper. if using loaf pans, bake at 350°F(180°c) for 40-50minutes. Be careful not to overbake; use a toothpick to test for doneness(it should come out clean with a few crumbs)
  6. Cool and Enjoy:Allow the cake to cool completely before silicing.Serve and savor the wonderful orange flavor!
